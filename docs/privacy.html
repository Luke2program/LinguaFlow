1. Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using our Service, I may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) with encryption in transit and at rest. All authentication is handled through industry-standard OAuth 2.0 and secure token-based systems.
